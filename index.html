🧳 Bag Strategy

🍼 PUMP BAG — EXEMPT

Medical device, does not count toward carry-on limits. Bring pump, all parts, bags, supplies.

👜 PERSONAL ITEM

Backpack under seat. Documents, tech, liquids bag, iPad, snacks, contacts, glasses.

🧳 CARRY-ON

Roller bag. All clothing, shoes, toiletries, meds. Wear sneakers on plane — 4 shoe pairs is tight, consider swapping crocs for sandals.

🔄 RETURN

Frozen milk goes in pump bag (cooler) + soft duffel if needed. Pack dry ice or gel packs. Breast milk can fly as carry-on or checked — no quantity limit.
